Activate the X-Ray Goggles

Click on the X-Ray Goggles in your bookmark toolbar to activate the Goggles. By the way: you can do this on any website!

To deactivate, just press 'esc' on your keyboard at any time

If you haven't installed the X-Ray Goggles yet, click here to go to the install page.

Learn how to enable your toolbar

Remix the Images

Hover over any of the animal sections (top, middle, or bottom) to highlight them with the Goggles, then click them to open their code in the Goggles editor.

Make a New Animal!

Play around by swapping the words dinosaur, fox, and bird in each section.

Click the Update button to save your changes.

(You can also use a completely different image! When using Goggles, you can add any image from anywhere on the Web, but be sure to respect copyright law: just you can find an image does not mean you're automatically free to use it for your own purposes!)

Remix the Words

Hover over this section of text to highlight it, and then click it to open the editor.

Introduce Your Animal to the World!

Delete the text between <h1> and </h1> and give your animal a name. Then, update the text between the <p> and </p> with some details about your animal. Where does it live? What does it eat? What kind of music does it like?

Click the "Update" button to save your changes.

Publish Your Animal

You're done! When you're ready to publish and share your animal, click on Publish.
