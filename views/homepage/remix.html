Use X-Ray Goggles to Teach

Use these activities to teach others how to code.

Hack the News

Learners will use X-Ray Goggles to remix a news website, learning about openly-licensed resources, different forms of media, and how to create something new on the Web through remix.

Try this activity

Hack My Media

The goal of this activity is for participants to think critically about their identities and the media they enjoy. They will use these reflections to create their first webpage by hacking and tinkering with an existing webpage.

Try this activity
